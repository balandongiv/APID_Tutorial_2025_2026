\section*{Large Language Model}
For this course, you are allowed to use LLM, in fact recommended to use. 

Of course, for identifying customer needs, it is better if you can talk directly with the customer, but, else, you can consider LLM as one of the alternative to extract the need. 
However, it is imperative for you to disclose this in your report writing (with video proof), as extra mark will be given for the extra effort. 

In fact, for writing or compiling the report, I do not have any issues with the usage of LLM as I am only concerned with the idea. And I will also evaluate your understanding most of the time verbally.
